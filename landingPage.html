Bob's Bagels

Freshly baked, filled and served to you daily

Baking bagels since 1998

Bob's Bagels have been rolling, boiling and delivering baked goods to Leeds cafes since 1998, but now you can pick up their incredible bagels at their very own Holmfirth cafe. Plain, poppy seed, sesame or pretzel salt? Once you’ve chosen your bagel, it’s time to fill it up. They do all the classics, from their triple nut peanut butter to the salmon and cream cheese. Fancy a classic favourite? The salt beef with gherkins and mustard is a must-try. Keep an eye out for their ever-changing array of daily specials, cakes and pretzels too.
